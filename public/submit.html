We Want to Know.

Every story on CreepyCoords was submitted by someone who experienced it firsthand. If you've encountered something you can't explain — a location, a road, a building, a ritual — tell us what happened. We investigate every submission.

📋 Submission Guidelines
  • Tell us exactly what you saw, heard, or felt — no embellishment needed
  • Include the specific location — address, city, and country if possible
  • Tell us when it happened — approximate date or year is fine
  • Photos and video are welcome but not required
  • Anonymous submissions are accepted — we never publish names without permission
  • We verify locations before publishing — fabricated submissions are not accepted
